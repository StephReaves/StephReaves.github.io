Week 4 Cultural

Gregorc Thinking Style:

My Gregorc thinking style is concrete random (11pts) followed by abstract random (10pts). I find this interesting that I would be so close on both abstract and concrete. I definitely agree that my first thoughts are geared towards being concrete versus being abstract. In general, with both there are points that I agree with and disagree with about my personality and thinking style.

I would agree with the preferences of Concrete Random of:

  • Experimenting to find answers
  • Taking risks
  • Using my intuition
  • Solving problems independently
So while at DBC I'll try to be authentic and upfront with my tendencies. Hopefully, this will allow me to find a subset of individuals that complement each other thinking styles. For instance, it would be good to have individuals with different thinking styles to create a diverse work group that will approach the various tasks effectively.

Growth vs. Fixed Mindset:

I am definitely more of a growth mindset type of person. However, I would say that I'm Fixed in a sense - Basically, I think that everyone has a maximum potential and that is fixed. However, everyone should work towards their full potential which makes me growth mindeset oriented.

I came across this CNN article, which I think does a great job of outlining how a growth vs. fixed mindset can impact an individual career and life trajectory. One of the big takeaways for me is that perserverance is a key trait to possess when tackling something new and challenging. At times the only thing that distinguishes someone that succeeds from someone that fails is perserverance. So as I continue on my coding journey, I will seek to bring 100% of my foucus and ability everyday to continue on the path to success.
